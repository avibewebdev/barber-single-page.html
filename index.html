Who We Are

"A haircut is more than a trim. It's a ritual."

El Camino Barbers has been the community's go-to barber shop since 2009, built on a foundation of skill, trust, and genuine connection.

The Story

From One Chair to a Community

Mr. T started cutting hair out of passion long before it became a profession. Growing up watching his grandfather barber in the neighborhood, he understood early that a barbershop is more than a place — it's a gathering ground.

In 2009, he opened the doors to El Camino Barbers with one chair and a vision. Today, that vision has grown into a full team of licensed professionals who share the same passion for the craft.

With more than 30 years of combined experience, our barbers are trained in everything from traditional clipper work to modern fades, classic straight razor shaves, and all hair types — ensuring every client leaves looking and feeling their best.

Our Journey

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment or can I walk in?
+

Walk-ins are always welcome during our business hours (Tuesday–Friday 10am–6:30pm, Saturday 10am–2pm). However, we recommend booking an appointment — especially on Fridays and Saturdays — to minimize your wait time and guarantee a spot with your preferred barber.

How long does a typical haircut take?
+

A classic haircut typically takes 25–35 minutes. Fades and tapers run closer to 40 minutes. Combined services like our Cut + Beard package take 50–60 minutes. The VIP Experience is approximately 75 minutes. Times may vary depending on hair length and desired style.

What payment methods do you accept?
+

We accept cash, all major credit and debit cards, Venmo, and Zelle. A 3% processing fee applies to card payments. Tipping your barber is always appreciated and can be done in any of these methods.

What is your cancellation policy?
+

We ask for at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a cancellation fee. We understand emergencies happen — just give us a call.

Do you cut all hair types?
+

Yes — our barbers are trained and experienced with all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and coily hair textures. We specialize in diverse hair types and can accommodate any style you're looking for. If you have any specific concerns, feel free to call ahead.

Do you offer kids haircuts?
+

Absolutely. We offer kids cuts for children 12 and under at a discounted rate. Alex is particularly great with younger clients. We recommend scheduling an appointment for kids visits to minimize wait time and make the experience as smooth as possible.

How often should I get a haircut?
+

For most clients, we recommend every 2–3 weeks to maintain a fresh look. If you have a skin fade, 2 weeks is ideal. For longer styles, every 4–6 weeks works well. Our Sharp Club membership is designed for clients who want to stay consistently sharp.

Is the shop accessible / ADA compliant?
+

Yes, our shop is ADA accessible. If you have any specific accessibility needs, please don't hesitate to call us ahead of your visit and we'll make sure everything is accommodated.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to help. Give us a call during business hours or send us a message.
